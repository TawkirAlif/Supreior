About Superior Mutual:

Superior Mutual is an independent agency that provides the quickest, hassle-free, and convenient way to buy final expense burial insurance. Its mission is to provide same-day, immediate coverage for adults in less than 30 minutes!

Our team of bi-lingual specialist will shop for the most competitive carriers to find the best values in life insurance. Just select the amount desired;we’ll place a conference call to the carrier and complete the application with a voice signature. It’s just that simple!

About Our Founder Al Kushner

Al Kushner is the Founder and CEO of Superior Mutual, an independent life insurance agency with expertise in final expense burial insurance for clients and their parents. He has been a licensed insurance agent since 1986.

For most of his entire career, Al has focused on final expense burial insurance. With over 35 years of experience, his wealth of knowledge has helped countless consumers across America get the most affordable coverage available.

He is the author of the best-selling book called “The Savvy Guide to Buying Burial Insurance” published in 2021 by SCR Media Inc. Available through Amazon, Walmart, Barnes & Noble, Apple Books, Kobo, Tolino, Overdrive, Biblioteca, Baker & Taylor, Vivlio and your local library upon request.
